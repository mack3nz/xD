The Importance of Cybersecurity (Cibersegurança)

Cybersecurity is essential in today’s digital world. As we depend more on the internet for communication, shopping, banking, and storing sensitive information, protecting our data has never been more critical.

Cyberattacks can cause data breaches, financial loss, and damage to a company’s reputation. Developers, companies, and users must follow good security practices to avoid these threats.

Security involves not only technology but also awareness and behavior. Using strong passwords, enabling two-factor authentication, and keeping systems updated are key actions.

With the rise of cloud computing and remote work, cybersecurity has become even more important, requiring constant monitoring and quick responses to threats.
